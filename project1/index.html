Let's Talk about Guitars

There are two main types of guitars:

  • Electric
  • Acoustic
Let's go into greater detail and discuss their differences.

Electric Guitars

An electric guitar is a guitar that uses a pickup to convert the vibration of its strings into electrical impulses.

Acoustic Guitars

An acoustic guitar is a guitar that uses only acoustic (as opposed to electronic) means to transmit the strings' vibrational energy to the air in order to produce a sound.
